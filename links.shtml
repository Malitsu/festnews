Finnish Cinema

Finnish Film Archive (FFA). A state institution that acquires and restores films and related material.
www.sea.fi/english

Finnish Society for Cinema Studies. The society promotes research in the field of films and audiovisual culture. Its members include professionals of cinema studies as well as students and teachers.
www.uta.fi/jarjestot/sets

Finnish Film Foundation. The task of the foundation is to support and promote Finnish film production. Information about grants provided by the foundation can be found on the site.
www.ses.fi

The Finnish Film Contact. The film distribution organization has a collection of over 600 films, including documentaries, children’s films, musicals, animations and experimental films.
www.elokuvakontakti.fi

Pirkanmaa Film Center. The Film Center aims at advancing knowledge of film culture in the Tampere region by organizing screenings and education.
www.elokuvakeskus.com/english

Oulu Film Center. A cinema organization situated in Northern Finland which organizes the International Children’s Film Festival annually.
http://www.ouka.fi/oek/in_english/index.html

Finnish Board of Film Classification. The Board classifies the films and audiovisual programs exhibited or distributed to persons under 18 years of age. The site contains information on legislation and the classification process.
www.vet.fi

The Finnish Film Chamber. Central organisation of Finnish film.
www.filmikamari.fi

The Union of Finnish Film and Video Employees.
www.teme.fi/set
