Festival News 2007

Festival News will be published on the web and also as a printed version during the Tampere Film Festival, 2007. The online version of Festival News comes out daily during the Festival from March 8th to March 12th. The printed version comes out twice, on Wednesday March 7th and Saturday March 10th.

Students of the Department of Journalism and Mass Communication are responsible for the articles, photos, web design and the layout of the newspaper. Students of the Department of Translation Studies do the translations.

This publication is a product of an academic journalism course. From the project, the students get work experience and credits for their studies.
